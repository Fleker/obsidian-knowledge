title: "Cultured Meat"
---
Use animal cells taken non-fatally and grown in a lab to produce the perfect meat. The same tech can be adapted to produce cultured milk and eggs.

## Companies
- Farmless is [a Dutch startup using fermentation technology](https://techcrunch.com/2023/12/04/farmless-alternative-protein-4m-food-tech/)

## Politics
- Disliked by [Fetterman and DeSantis](https://twitter.com/SenFettermanPA/status/1786064644383142187?t=R8hmI2gGIxrZVBU8s6jqSg&s=19)
- Several states like Mississippi are [banning this](https://www.nytimes.com/2025/03/14/science/whos-afraid-of-lab-grown-meat.html)

## Economics
- As it scales, chicken will be able to get in the range of [$6.20/lb](https://x.com/cremieuxrecueil/status/1827848463603925387)
