In Sweden, adding bike lanes [led to a 20% increase in biking](https://x.com/DavidZipper/status/1801230693055201788). They [reduce congestion](https://x.com/DavidZipper/status/1851958151542661538).

Good video [from Quebec Mayor](https://x.com/f0rmat/status/1801885057440735379?t=EKOXD-ShSRru8bp2lTmdeg&s=19)

In Bayside, bike lanes [are weirdly controversial](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/07/01/nyregion/street-wars-bike-lanes-queens.html). Probably due to removing parking?

* A buffer zone between parking and bikes p[can be safer](https://x.com/Boenau/status/1991532486376230959)) particularly if they’re on the passenger side
* 6K cyclists [per hour]\(https://x.com/headwaysmatter/status/1998034027673231689) in key NYC lanes
* Voters don’t seem [to punish politicians](https://x.com/jarjoh/status/1966125346744631395) for adding bike lanes
* Study shows how they [improve safety for everyone on the road](https://x.com/Boenau/status/2014015130154000513) 
* A plumber explains [how he uses bikes for most of his business](https://x.com/Boenau/status/1911071104875090281) 


## In New York City
- Erik Bottcher [added a bike lane](https://x.com/ebottcher/status/1823501670786994360) to 10th Ave in <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Manhattan</span>
- DOT is building a bike lane [from Dean Street to Flatbush](https://x.com/ammorano/status/1833529394624926065)
- DOT is working on expanding [concrete lanes](https://x.com/vinbarone/status/1831706236879802416)
- Federal funding secured for [a west-east bike connector](https://x.com/SenSchumer/status/1837217773174378509?t=O6rykknbToXJVNnDlpkGiA&s=19) in Inwood
* [@sam_d_1995 highlights how well-protected bike lanes can serve as emergency lanes to bypass gridlock and reduce response times.](https://x.com/sam_d_1995/status/2062527682185719834)
* [Streetfilms highlights the stark contrast in urban space efficiency by comparing how the same curb space allotment can accommodate either 8 to 10 parked cars, 88 Citibikes, or hundreds of delivery e-bikes.](https://x.com/Streetfilms/status/2047673308783493564)


## In Philadelphia
- Philly Council President proposed [**Get out of the Bike Lane**](https://x.com/DPearsonPHL/status/1831729257137000637) bill to increase scrutiny for cars parking in bike lanes

## Safety
- Study showed separated bike lanes [reduce car speeds](https://x.com/grescoe/status/1840737965384843569[Sixth Ave bike lane](https://x.com/StreetsblogNYC/status/1841218060000063957)
- One teen charged with murder [when he, on video, commits to killing cyclist](https://x.com/Boenau/status/1884257943132229676)
- In Manhattan, bike lanes [on avenues](https://x.com/vinbarone/status/1897399793749778775) led to 18% fewer injuries, 29% for pedestrians, and 39% for seniors

## Economics

* [Investing in pedestrian-friendly infrastructure like bike lanes and public seating can revitalize commercial areas, as demonstrated by the decrease in storefront vacancies on Hudson Street after a streetscape revamp.](https://x.com/vinbarone/status/2032109447640670283)


## Politics

* [Daniel Pearson (@DPearsonPHL) highlights that despite vocal anti-bike lane sentiment, political and policy realities consistently lead elected officials to support cycling infrastructure.](https://x.com/DPearsonPHL/status/2059342828501630989)
* [Ryan (@ryanphanna) highlights the lack of empirical evidence from critics who claim that installing bike lanes causes traffic congestion.](https://x.com/ryanphanna/status/2062402264858329252)
