A household or industrial machine which used additive manufacturing to produce a good.

It can be used with plastic [or glass](https://youtu.be/0QEAuoSxZNc?si=DTo5uJJBUIsjwK2x).

## Cool Prints
- [ForgeCore](https://thangs.com/designer/ForgeCore) does plants and tabletop games
- For tabletop games, you can do 3d-printed [terrains and scenery](https://www.printablescenery.com/)
- Pieces for [Axis & Allies](https://www.thingiverse.com/thing:4197696)
	- [War game pieces](https://www.thingiverse.com/m_bergman/designs) by m_bergman

## Scanning
You can use OSS tools like [OpenScan](https://openscan.eu/). They [can produce reasonably good models](https://www.reddit.com/r/PrintedMinis/comments/sis33t/3d_scanned_a_28mm_printed_mini_with_my_3d_printed/?utm_medium=android_app&utm_source=share).

## Impressive Projects

## Law & Politics

* [Kitten (@kitten_beloved) highlights the duality of 3D printing as a revolutionary tool for makers—capable of creating functional items like firearms—versus a source of useless plastic waste for average consumers.](https://x.com/kitten_beloved/status/2046310563312197803)
* [Lukas Ziegler showcases Functgraph, an innovative 3D printer and assembly station developed at Meiji University that autonomously prints, cleans, and assembles its own tools to perform physical tasks like a mini-factory.](https://x.com/lukas_m_ziegler/status/2049024010214601118)
