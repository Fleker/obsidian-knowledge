Misanthropes who pull whatever they want to reject any new housing, affordable or market-rate, in their neighborhood.

Many NIMBYs own property and use that as a reason to fight against new development. Of course, property owners [tend to live in expensive property](https://twitter.com/GeorgistSteve/status/1742079961333424299) and this generally leads them to become rent seekers of land, something disliked under a [Land Value Tax](./Land%20Value%20Tax.md.md).

There is an inherent tension [between homeowners who want house prices to rise](https://t.co/Fbc4MfvFzE) and new buyers/renters.

An op-ed in 1978 from SF notes how [the downzoning would hurt people](https://x.com/maxdubler/status/1778849205064544586):
> Downzonings "cut out low & moderate income" "saved the whales, fishes, birds, & bees, but forgot its people"

NIMBY policy [is conservative-coded](https://x.com/cafedujord/status/1821018178656534877). Trump [is running on NIMBYism](https://x.com/JoshEakle/status/1822358965327454627?t=xNyZLlLtRqqnz4Fowc1Qqw&s=19).

A lot of zoning was developed for [explicit racial segregation](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/8cd986b3c5ab4f1c8bedba85f195662f).

People will pay $9500 for a house 0.5 fewer units/ac1re, about $4500 on average [for less density](https://x.com/lymanstoneky/status/1851258353386672394).

NIMBYism has resulted in [a 8.9% reduction in GDP](https://x.com/IterIntellectus/status/1875669529151598881) and a loss of $16K a person.

The instance they buy a house [they become a NIMBY](https://x.com/MRJKilcoyne/status/1896595150375436411).

NIMBYism is common [across the Anglosphere](https://x.com/StatisticUrban/status/1942145493896950195).

* [According to 404 Media, a local township voted to place a 365-day hold on providing water to a planned AI data center for America's nuclear weapons scientists.](https://www.instapaper.com/read/2007841669)
