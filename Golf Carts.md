This is an interesting intracity transit idea that is similar to [E-Bikes](./E-Bikes.md) in that they're electric. They're a bit larger. They are popular transportation alternatives to large vehicles and SUVs.

In <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">New Orleans</span>, the city is [cracking down on carts](https://twitter.com/DavidZipper/status/1788278769356017738).
