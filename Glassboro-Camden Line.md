title: "Glassboro-Camden Line"
---
A piecemeal [Heavy Rail](./Heavy%20Rail.md) transit system that is meant to connect <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Rowan University</span> in <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Glassboro</span> with <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Camden</span>, enabling students and residents to transfer to other rail systems nearby. A spiritual successor to the [Pennsylvania-Reading Seashore Line](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pennsylvania-Reading_Seashore_Lines#:~:text=The%20Pennsylvania%2DReading%20Seashore%20Lines,Railroad%20and%20the%20Reading%20Company.).

See https://www.glassborocamdenline.com/.

- It is [going to happen](https://www.nj.com/news/2025/12/controversial-2b-nj-rail-project-moving-forward-despite-residents-saying-no.html?utm_source=twitter&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=njdotcom_sf&utm_content=nj_twitter_njdotcom) despite some opposition
