These are a new class of drugs, originally designed for diabetes. Then studies showed how they have an additional weight loss effect, kicking off a new generation of biotech.

Semaglutides.

* [Evis Drenova highlights how the rise of highly effective GLP-1 obesity drugs like Ozempic disproves the long-held theory that big pharma would suppress such treatments to protect downstream chronic disease profits.](https://x.com/evisdrenova/status/2058410171651834356)

## Addiction
One study finds a 50% reduction in alcohol-use disorder [compared to Naltrexone](https://twitter.com/StatisticUrban/status/1796848871294796191).

## Organs
> A major clinical trial has shown that semaglutide, the active ingredient in the popular drugs Ozempic and Wegovy, significantly reduced the risk of kidney complications, heart issues, and death in patients with Type 2 diabetes and chronic kidney disease. -[NYT](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/05/24/well/ozempic-kidney-disease-semaglutide.html?utm_source=substack&utm_medium=email)

There's growing evidence that it [can help with neurological diseases](https://x.com/ScienceMagazine/status/1816398064011083922).

## Broadly Health
- They are less likely to [die of COVID or other ailments](https://x.com/MattZeitlin/status/1829650604253462941)
- Eases [knee osteoarthritis pain](https://www.nytimes.com/2024/10/30/health/ozempic-wegovy-knee-pain-osteoarthritis.html)
- One paper notes an [11% decline](https://x.com/JuraWho/status/1874112741977591839) in savory snacks
	- > “Households with at least one GLP-1 user reduce grocery spending by approximately 6% within six months of adoption, with higher-income households reducing spending by nearly 9%.”
* [Nicholas Decker (@captgouda24) notes that Ozempic, a weight loss drug, is significantly reducing demand for food, impacting consumer purchasing patterns.](https://x.com/captgouda24/status/1931428568313282621)
