---
The principle of <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Jus Soli</span> in the US.

- In Lynch v Clarke (1844) it confirmed that children born of business travelers were considered citizens
- Trump tried to end it via executive action and [it was blocked by a judge](https://x.com/micah_erfan/status/1882544962447056992)
	- Is blocked [by putting everyone in a class action](https://x.com/NewsWire_US/status/1943312418022588657) lawsuit
- Without it, we end up with an [intergenerational ethnic underclass](https://x.com/ASPertierra/status/1939719974739693593)
* [The U.S. Conference of Catholic Bishops filed an amicus brief with the Supreme Court opposing President Trump's order to end birthright citizenship, calling it immoral and urging the court to protect human dignity.](https://x.com/KelseyReichmann/status/2027133247302377802)
