These can also be known as "granny flats" or guest homes. These are structures that can exist on a residential plot of land that give one some amount of autonomy while also having cheaper housing costs.

## Granny Towers
San Diego regulations allow for [virtually unlimited housing units](https://www.worksinprogress.news/p/the-san-diego-infinite-housing-glitch) with some stipulations.
