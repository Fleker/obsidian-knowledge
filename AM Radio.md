title: "AM Radio"
---

Amplitude modulation allows for the broadcast of speech on radio frequencies. It's not as noiseless as FM radio but uses less energy and can broadcast voice over long ranges, making it useful for emergencies.

The [AM for Every Vehicle Act](https://www.theverge.com/2024/9/18/24248137/am-radio-bill-house-energy-commerce-ev-interference) will mandate all cars, including EVs, to have AM radios.
