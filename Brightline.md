## Collisions

- Brightline trains have [killed 182 people](https://x.com/rjwile/status/1945095766500106394) due to poor driving
- Local towns don't want to have horns and have [bad road design](https://x.com/watn_tarnation/status/1945488515418276265)
- Drivers will [just go around crossing gates](https://x.com/ArmandDoma/status/1945496150435348624)
