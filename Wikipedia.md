* [Oliver Jia highlights that Japanese Wikipedia lacks images compared to its English counterpart because Japan has no concept of "fair use," reflecting strict copyright laws and cultural differences in IP ownership.](https://x.com/OliverJia1014/status/2035559721684566116)
* [Riley Walz (@rtwlz) created a "Wikipedia Time Machine" tool that allows users to travel back in time and view historical revisions of Wikipedia articles as they appeared on any specific date.](https://x.com/rtwlz/status/2048793490017145230)
* [Nav Toor highlights Halupedia, an open-source project that uses AI to generate a completely hallucinated, Wikipedia-like encyclopedia in real-time as users visit it.](https://x.com/heynavtoor/status/2054208239282356304)
