When sports betting becomes legal, [bankrupcies increase 28%](https://x.com/ArmandDoma/status/1816499655221694722). They [reduce household investment by 14%](https://x.com/florianederer/status/1825052531304579341). And [partner violence](https://x.com/mikekofoed/status/1869368619387531656) dramatically spikes.

In Brazil, it's been [even worse](https://x.com/TheStalwart/status/1856676464193617943), with excessive credit being pulled.

In Massachusetts, they're paying a non-profit to help deal with the addiction problem. That non-profit [is now paying for gambling ads](https://x.com/sunraysunray/status/1870083868042068155).

- DraftKings is planning [a $20/mo subscription service](https://x.com/Phil_Lewis_/status/1875590398124634262) to give increased odds on bets that profit the company.
- 13M Americans [bet over $500/month in 2024](https://x.com/ButlerBets/status/1889402377918206375)
