Cars kill people, and that's bad! Many drivers die in car accidents, and pedestrian deaths are on the rise.

Part of this rise is due to a lack of traffic enforcement. People who have multiple speeding and/or parking violations should have their license suspended and their car impounded. People who do not have a license or use fake tags should be sent to jail.
