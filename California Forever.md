A proposed city north of <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">San Francisco</span> in <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">California</span>. It is meant to be a new planned city with a lot of support from tech entrepreneurs.

Nolan Gray [examines the controversies](https://x.com/mnolangray/status/1803322692256584048) around the project. It does not use environmentally risky land. At the moment the space is used for livestock grazing (not very environmental).

* [American business and labor have formed a historic partnership to construct a new city in California, with groundbreaking planned for 2026, representing a significant investment and economic opportunity.](https://x.com/jansramek/status/2013994444681593140)
* [Devon Zuegel is developing Esmeralda, a new walkable town in California inspired by Chautauqua, aiming to foster a multi-generational community focused on learning, sustainability, and a vibrant culture of invention.](https://www.instapaper.com/read/1874735589)
* [Jan Sramek's California Forever project proposes building a massive new planned city in Solano County, sparking strong opposition from local Rio Vista residents eager to protect their rural community.](https://www.instapaper.com/read/1993207470)


## Urban Philosophy
They want a minimum density of 20 units/acre. There is no train to SF in the initial planning. That's more of a 20-year project.

## Suisun Expansion

Rather than a charter city, they are [glomping on](https://www.suisun.com/files/sharedassets/suisuncity/v/1/training/resources/images/michaels-images/documents/c.-suisun-expansion-specific-plan.pdf) to expand Suisun.
