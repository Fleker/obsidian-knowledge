America Track, a public [Heavy Rail](./Heavy%20Rail.md.md) service that lines the country. When the railroads were nationalized in 1971, [many small lines shut down](https://x.com/ThunderWolf08/status/1809969965530796102).

Amtrak usage [is increasing with more service](https://twitter.com/ThunderWolf08/status/1780959346115084695?t=eeaG2IZXuXDwao-Ze-Zc_g&s=19). And it's [setting ridership records](https://twitter.com/ThunderWolf08/status/1786408955138183550?t=gmRp-M1pAa3HGO_jMbVkkA&s=19). It reached [its busiest year](https://x.com/ThunderWolf08/status/1861009650419634303) in 2024.

They added the [Borealis route](https://twitter.com/Amtrak/status/1785698197865705860?t=M83Ru61cJjLTN2y841_UKQ&s=19) from Chicago to St. Paul on <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">2024-05-01</span>. They are planning on adding a Japanese bullet train [between Houston and Dallas](https://twitter.com/Amtrak/status/1785021560580870558?t=iv5WGBS8cnug9lWAFZ3TEw&s=19).

On <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">2024-10-29</span> they [announced more grants](https://x.com/ThunderWolf08/status/1851301871547531641) including a new bridge in Albany and bringing Caltrain further into <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">San Francisco</span>. Then they announced [even more grants](https://x.com/ThunderWolf08/status/1857473645452419426) for general repairs to improve speed and service.

## South Carolina
Charlotte is [acquiring a railroad](https://twitter.com/JoeBrunoWSOC9/status/1795937377350828538) ROW from Norfolk Southern. This will allow them to build [their Red Line](https://x.com/the_transit_guy/status/1831320990019949025).

## S-Line
