## ADUs
Accessory dwelling units are sometimes called Granny Flats. Basically they are a guest house you can put on your property by-right. This increases your property value and that can disincentive building them. Under a regime with a [Land Value Tax](./Land%20Value%20Tax.md) that wouldn't be an issue.

ADUs can house additional people including renting out cheap space to guests.

https://www.construction-physics.com/p/could-we-boost-housing-construction

### Filtering
When middle class people move to new homes, the older homes now become available for people to live in. This concept is known as Filtering.

Even luxury homes allows [a chain of moving](https://twitter.com/GeorgistSteve/status/1740933623103238186?t=f3Tilsm1SIBeUWiYFwJJiw&s=19) which benefits poor people the most.

A new apartment in Berkeley is renting out [for $2295](https://x.com/Jeffinatorator/status/1828523347833201073) where an old apartment would've been $2500 a few years ago. But now that there is some relief, those old apartments are going to be rentable for cheaper.

### Rent Control
Rent control at first seems like a good solution. However, it penalizes new tenants by giving them [a much higher cost of rent](https://twitter.com/Sam_Dumitriu/status/1731641928322609562) to compensate. It doesn't necessarily restrict future building but does improperly shift the incentives in favor of existing people. This can lead to cementing of dynasties as the rent control status passes down to wealthy children who continue to abuse the system.

Real world cases show that rent control in St. Paul [led to greater rent increases compared to YIMBY Minneapolis](https://twitter.com/jessekb/status/1743355526090514755?t=MUYRMKYkQ2doTy-Erhy9Dg&s=19).

- It can [lead many apartments to be empty](https://twitter.com/scottlincicome/status/1754555672354587007) as they need expensive maintenance

### Housing Lottery
A housing lottery always implies there is not enough housing for everyone. This can lead to [multi-decade waits](https://www.bbc.com/worklife/article/20160517-this-is-one-city-where-youll-never-find-a-home) just for a place to stay. Housing delayed is housing denied.

One housing lottery on the UWS [has just 17 affordable units available](https://newyorkyimby.com/2024/07/housing-lottery-launches-for-165-west-80th-street-hdfc-co-ops-on-manhattans-upper-west-side.html).

## Urban Investments
When you have more residents, you have a broader tax base. This allows the city budgets to rise and help keep schools open and services available.

Seattle is [seeking to close many schools](https://twitter.com/jarjoh/status/1788416786850853238) due to budgetary issues.
