* [A 3-year basic income experiment in Germany with 122 participants aged 21-40 showed that recipients experienced increased happiness, improved health, higher savings, more generosity, and maintained or increased social engagement without reducing work hours.](https://x.com/scottsantens/status/1909945967152857403)
- Not many positive effects [in Sweden and Finland](https://x.com/CameronCorduroy/status/1998307254307824006) including no more crime

## Political Effects
- Made people [more resentful](https://x.com/dbroockman/status/1863628335248785783) that others wouldn't deserve it, and they'd support work requirements [for Medicaid](https://x.com/mattyglesias/status/1863644935875883465)
