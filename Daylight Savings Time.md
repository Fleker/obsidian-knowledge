title: "Daylight Savings Time"
---
A way to preserve daylight during the summer. It adds [40 minutes of waking daylight](https://x.com/NateSilver538/status/1869053902387315071) per year. If the [timezone borders shifted](https://x.com/StatisticUrban/status/1868175936002429092) it might not be as jarring.

<span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Mexico</span> recently [ended daylight saving time](https://x.com/ddiamond/status/1870486236848378312) and people seem in favor of it.
