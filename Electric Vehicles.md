Private automobiles that use batteries and electric motors.

## Charging
- There are [61K EV chargers compared to 150K gas stations, not including homes](https://x.com/johnrhanger/status/1793979621828178370?t=qMKuzQ5QWPlZhGRupkhjLA&s=19)
- Federal laws ban EV chargers at highway rest stops due to [a ban on commercial activity at rest stops](https://x.com/venerable_bede/status/1843736496035246096)
* [Electric vehicle charging times are rapidly improving, with new chargers like BYD's Flash Charging Battery enabling 400km of range in just five minutes, comparable to refueling a gasoline car.](https://x.com/balajis/status/2003072457473990710)

## Manufacturing
- [Old abandoned factories getting turned into EV factories with $2bn](https://www.theverge.com/2024/7/11/24195811/biden-ev-factory-retrofit-conversion-grant-funding)

## Consumer Adoption
- The pace of adoption [is rapid](https://x.com/Tyler_The_Wise/status/1807478242003329143)
	- In the US, [it's still growing](https://x.com/jmhorp/status/1876287678892909017)
- In Norway, EVs are [94% of vehicles sold in August 2024](https://x.com/drvolts/status/1830697236763943347)
- A Bloomberg presentation [suggests good growth](https://x.com/colinmckerrache/status/1800900721761821066)
	- Oil demand [seems to be hitting peak in a few years](https://x.com/colinmckerrache/status/1800902805156397315)
* [Matthew Yglesias notes that US gasoline consumption is declining, as visualized by a decreasing trend in barrels per day, likely influenced by the rise of electric vehicles and improved fuel efficiency.](https://x.com/mattyglesias/status/2009820819136856509)
* [EVwire.com's Jaan reports that combustion car sales have peaked globally and will not recover, supported by a chart showing declining gas car sales and rising EV adoption worldwide.](https://x.com/TheEVuniverse/status/2009184565575995819)
