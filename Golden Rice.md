Golden Rice is a kind of <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">GMO</span> which brings out a lot of Vitamin A, which gives it a golden color. This is important in some countries that are undernourished.

Unfortunately, Greenpeace [sued to stop its implementation](https://t.co/qV8RZyryHd) in the Phillipines.

* [The Philippines has approved a genetically modified high-iron rice variety (HIZ039) that could significantly combat iron deficiency anemia, a widespread issue in developing countries.](https://x.com/dylanmatt/status/2055004274447253989)
* [Anthropocene Magazine reports that researchers have successfully bred a gene from wild rice into domestic crops to create a perennial rice that regrows after harvest, a major step toward reducing agriculture's climate impact.](https://www.instapaper.com/read/2001822739)
