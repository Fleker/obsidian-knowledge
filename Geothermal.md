## Benefits
America has ["pioneered technologies like fracking and horizontal drilling that allow us to dig deeper and create reservoirs."](https://substack.com/app-link/post?publication_id=232077&post_id=143896300&utm_source=post-email-title&utm_campaign=email-post-title&isFreemail=false&r=7zeyi&token=eyJ1c2VyX2lkIjoxMzQwOTY1OCwicG9zdF9pZCI6MTQzODk2MzAwLCJpYXQiOjE3MTM5MDI3NjYsImV4cCI6MTcxNjQ5NDc2NiwiaXNzIjoicHViLTIzMjA3NyIsInN1YiI6InBvc3QtcmVhY3Rpb24ifQ.-xsrOll_xKWHLQmrRarL3krgOEyXBmDEMe2z2jL6GoQ)

With deep enough wells, geothermal can effectively be used anywhere in the world.

There's an estimated [5.5TW of available geothermal](https://www.niskanencenter.org/geothermal-is-hot-on-capitol-hill/?utm_source=feedly&utm_medium=rss&utm_campaign=geothermal-is-hot-on-capitol-hill).

## Regulatory Environment
The Bureau of Land Management has granted a [categorical exemption](https://www.blm.gov/press-release/blm-adopts-categorical-exclusions-expedite-geothermal-energy-permitting?utm_source=substack&utm_medium=email) for geothermal explorations on public land.

There is also [bipartisan interest](https://www.lee.senate.gov/2024/3/lee-introduces-bipartisan-legislation-to-accelerate-adoption-of-geothermal-energy?utm_source=substack&utm_medium=email) in improving the permitting process for advanced geothermal.

> Geothermal production can require as many as six <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">NEPA</span> (National Environmental Policy Act) reviews for a single project.
