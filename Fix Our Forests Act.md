A bill proposed by Representative Scott Peters which will make it easier for the government [to clear dry brush](https://x.com/offbeatorbit/status/1876697228150198392).

The <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Sierra Club</span> and other environmental orgs [oppose the bill](https://x.com/ChadPergram/status/1882444010888827021).
