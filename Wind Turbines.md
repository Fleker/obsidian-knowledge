A form of <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Clean Energy</span> which uses wind to spin a turbine to generate electricity. Today, we now regularly have [21 MW offshore wind turbines](https://x.com/SimonMahan/status/1800633330867589203).

Cats kill [orders of magntidue](https://x.com/jamieclimate/status/1806304418146468320) more birds than turbines.

Both Kentucky and Tennessee [have bans on wind energy](https://x.com/ArmandDoma/status/1864366493418574195), meaning expanding clean energy would be a form of deregulation.
