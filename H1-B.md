This is a program for skilled <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Immigration</span>. This is different [from H2-B](https://x.com/DanielDiMartino/status/1874990927577850230) Visas which are for seasonal guest workers.

## Requirements
- Theoretical or practical application of knowledge in a specialized field
- A bachelor's degree or higher in that field
	- Cooks getting $30K [are not getting H1-B Visas](https://x.com/ArmandDoma/status/1872758639074918618)
- Employers must pay [about $17K in legal fees](https://x.com/cojobrien/status/1874859062699176430) in addition to paying employees the salary
- Only [85K applicants](https://x.com/deedydas/status/1872119397663285267) are available each year

## Employee Conditions
- Most Tesla employees on H1-Bs [make 6 figures](https://x.com/SpeakSamuel/status/1877155415152329141)
- Registered nurses don't [quality for H1-B](https://x.com/SpeakSamuel/status/1874978548626112777) Visas due to educational requirements
- H1-B Visas [can be transferred between employers](https://x.com/micah_erfan/status/1875036927092777041)
	- It [takes a few steps](https://x.com/micah_erfan/status/1875036932159459409)
- Netflix employees [make good money, six figures](https://x.com/Tyler_The_Wise/status/1872311512062427456)
