title: "Heavy Rail"
---

Big, thick trains which are setup to travel long distances .

They support high capacity, [equivalent to 14 lanes of traffic](https://x.com/harshahahaaha/status/1824319427292139990).

Sometimes they use diesel. But if they switch to electrification, [they can go faster and accelerate faster](https://x.com/mnolangray/status/1827111279158554837). This will allow the <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Caltrain</span> to [reduce headways and reduce travel time](https://x.com/seungylee14/status/1828466399075074214).
