title: "California Impact Fees"
---
A rather unproductive tax on construction, which is partially used to raise municipal revenue since <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">Prop 13</span> makes it tough to raise it in the standard course of things.

They might charge [over $100k](https://twitter.com/maxdubler/status/1778841932141408432) such as in Sunnyvale, which certainly impacts the ability to build and purchase new housing.

This was [ruled unconstitutional by the Supreme Court](https://twitter.com/louismirante/status/1778794716718584078) in a unanimous decision on <span class="private-link" title="This page is private" style="color: #94a3b8; cursor: not-allowed; text-decoration: dashed underline;">2024-04-12</span>.
